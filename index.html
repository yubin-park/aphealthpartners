About Us

AP Health Partners is formed by a group of healthcare investors, physicians, and technology professionals to transform healthcare delivery. Our mission is to allow physicians to focus on high-quality clinical care and not time-consuming administrative matters. We are flexible in our partnership approach. Our goal is to develop long-term relations and support the long-term growth of your practice.

Partnership

We provide turn-key Chronic Condition Management services, Remote Patient Monitoring services, Value-based Care analysis with our proprietary technology tools. We will strengthen your business with substantial revenue growth, and help you transition to Value-based Care.

Joint Venture

On top of our partnership offerings, we bring capital to help you focus on clinical aspects. We provide full administrative and business support e.g. HR, IT, billing, sales & marketing.

Acquisition

We provide capital with flexible terms at attractive valuations. You will have options to become a local board advisor to our company, and continue seeing patients part-time.
